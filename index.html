What Is Numerology?

Numerology is the mystical study of numbers and their profound influence on human life. Rooted in ancient traditions from Pythagoras to the Kabbalah, it reveals the invisible patterns that shape your destiny.

1

Your Name

Every letter in your name carries a specific vibrational frequency. The Pythagorean chart maps A–Z to numbers 1–9.

2

Your Birthdate

Your birth day, month, and year reduced to a single digit (or master number) reveals your Life Path — the road you are meant to walk.

3

Core Numbers

Soul Urge, Personality, Destiny, and Personal Year numbers form a complete map of your inner world and outward journey.

4

Your Blueprint

Combining all core numbers creates a uniquely personal cosmic blueprint — your complete numerological profile for self-mastery.

Life Path Number

Your most significant number, derived from your birthdate. It describes your overall journey, innate talents, and the lessons you are here to master in this lifetime.

Soul Urge Number

Derived from the vowels in your name, the Soul Urge reveals your innermost desires, hidden motivations, and what truly makes your heart sing at the deepest level.

Personality Number

Computed from consonants in your name, this number reveals the mask you show the world — how others perceive you and the first impression you naturally project.

Numerology operates in 9-year cycles. Your Personal Year number pinpoints the theme and energy for any given year, helping you make aligned decisions and navigate timing.
